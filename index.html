FAQ

Do I need a minimum number of followers?

No. Musin is open to creators of all sizes — from small niche pages to large audiences.

Which platforms are supported?

Currently Musin supports: TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Spotify, SoundCloud, Stripe.

When do I get paid?

Payouts are released after your post is approved by the campaign. Payments are sent directly to your Stripe account.

Do I have to post every track I accept?

No. Only accept tracks you genuinely want to use in your content.

Can I choose the music myself?

Yes. You receive or browse music and choose tracks that fit your style, audience, and content.

Is it free?

Yes. Creating an account and browsing campaigns is completely free.

How are payouts handled?

All payouts are processed securely through Stripe and sent in your local currency where supported.

Can artists or labels contact me directly?

Yes. Some music campaigns can be offered to you directly from artists, labels, or music teams interested in your content style.
